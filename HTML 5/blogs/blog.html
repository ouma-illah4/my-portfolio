The Hidden Treasure of the Word

August 20, 2025

“The kingdom of heaven is like treasure hidden in a field...” (Matt. 13:44). In this study, I reflect on how pursuing God’s Word brings true joy and value that surpasses all else.

Just like the man who sold everything to buy the field, we are called to give our all to Christ. True treasure is not in material wealth but in knowing God and walking in His Word daily.

May this post encourage you to dig deep into Scripture and discover the priceless treasure of God’s truth.
